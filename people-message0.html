JUDITH SOERYADJAYA

Chairman

ENDEAVOUR TO BUILD THE FUTURE

After completing her education in France and USA, she returned to Indonesia and joined the family business at Astra International. For over twelve years, her illustrious track record includes successfully growing Astra International to become the largest integrated automotive company in South East Asia. Mdm Judith play key roles in establishing successful companies such as Astra Otoparts, Astrindo Travel, Emeralda Golf Estate, Medistra Hospital, Mobil 88, Federal Oil, Mitra Pinasthika Mustika and many others.

In the mid 1980â€™s, Mdm Judith established Credo Group as an investment holding company for Astra International prior to its initial public listing in 1990, and she continue to guide Astra International to become the most valuable business enterprise in Indonesia.

Following the divestment of Astra International in 1992, Mdm Judith focus her energy into growing Credo Group portfolio of companies, such as Melia Hotels in Asia Pacific, Amandari hotel in Bali, G-Resources, Laundrette, Shacman, and many others.

Despite all the success in her businesses, she also spend considerable time in philanthropy work, through Reach Out Foundation, that build schools and provide free education to the less fortunate.

This selfless act allow Mdm Judith to continue the Soeryadjaya family legacy of giving back to the Indonesian society, by having its future generation ready to contribute to the Country. For more than 30 years she has continued to hold a prominent and highly respected position in the business community in Indonesia through her various ventures in:

Automotive Manufacture
Mining
Oil and gas
Health Care
Property development Services
Trading and finance.
