ABOUT US

Credo Group is an investment holding company established by Madam Judith Soeryadjaya, who is the youngest daughter of the late Mr William Soeryadjaya. Mr William Soeryadjaya is a highly respected businessman who founded Astra International in 1957, and successfully led the company to become the largest and most valuable diversified business group in South East Asia.
