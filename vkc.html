VERDANTURA KALTIM COAL

Through its subsidiary company, PT Verdantura Kaltim Coal (VKC), Credo Group expanded its business portfolio in 2009 to coal mine as the majority share- holder of a number of coal concessions in Kalimantan and Sumatera. The total 17 greenfield coal mine assets occupy a total area of approximately 95.000 Hectares with clean and clear certification by the Republic of Indonesia Ministry of Energy and Mineral Resources.

Under the current exploration results, VKC had estimated a potential of more than 100 million tons of coal resources potential ranging from low rank to ther- mal coal. Each asset is managed by an individual operating company as the concession proprietor responsible to execute an integrated exploration program in order to achieve strong outcome and

In addition to coal producer and to address the ever changing local and global economy, VKC is also exploring the potential in venturing with prospective part- ners to become an Independent Power Producer utilizing the company’s owned coal resources.
