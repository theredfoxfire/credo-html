MELIA - KUALA LUMPUR

The Gran Meliá Kuala Lumpur is situated in the centre of the financial and entertainment (“Golden Triangle”) of the Capital City of Malaysia. The hotel is conveniently accessible through a highway from the Kuala Lumpur International Airport.

EXCELLENCE, ELEGANT AND COMFORT Being awarded with ISO 9002 for excellent services in the hospitality industry, the hotel guarantees the best of services and comfort for a memorable stay to each of its guests. Its 277 Deluxe and Superior rooms, 21 Super Deluxe rooms, 1 Executive Suite and 3 Junior Suites are set to accommo- date every guest with the ultimate in luxury and comfort. Room facilities include individually controlled air-conditioning, 24-hour satellite TV programs, personalized voice mail system, data port in all suites and super deluxe rooms, mini-bar and coffee/tea making facilities. In addition to those guestrooms facilities, the Gran Meliá Kuala Lumpur maintains conference, meeting and wedding banquet facilities, which can support any corporate or social event with up to 400 participants. Its well-equipped business centre also provides full secretarial supports to cater to all business needs.

DELICACIES With exotic flavors of the Orient and intriguing selection of Mediterranean cuisine, Meliá Kuala Lumpur caters all tastes and palates at its “El Patio” Coffee House, “Goya” Mediterranean Restaurant and the “Fortune Palace” Chinese Restaurant, which is famous with its Dim Sum, Cantonese and Sze Chuan specialties.

OTHER FACILITIES A swimming pool, a relaxation centre and a live-music at lounge complement the ultimate comforts to the guests.
