AMANDARI

Amandari resort, which is situated on the verdant hills of Ubud, was opened in 1989. This name in the local dialect means “peaceful spirit”. Perched on an escarpment high above the picturesque Ayung River gorge in the village of Kedewatan, the resort was designed after a Balinese village, complete with its river stone walkways, shrines, gardens and high paras-stone walls. In Travel and Leisure magazine published in June 2005, Amandari was ranked first in Asia for Service amongst the World's Best Hotels for Service. Again, in 2006 Zagat Survey of Top International Resorts & Spas awarded Aman- dari with the Top Destination Spa.

Reflecting the elements of Balinese culture from its unique traditional beliefs and lifestyle to its remarkable artistry, Amandari’s ambience accentuates Bali's peaceful spirit and the mystical cycles of ritual expression.

Amandari is the perfect place to feel a harmony and beauty of true nature.

ACCOMMODATIONS Village Suites. There are two available types of village suite: a large combined bedroom and living room; or a ground floor living room with a separate queen-sized bedroom on a mezzanine level. Each suite is equipped with Javanese marble flooring, a bar, music system and an outdoor sunken marble bath.

Valley Suites. These suites are identical to Village Suites with an addition of panoramic view of either the Ayung Valley or surrounding paddy fields.

Pool Suites. Each of these suites has a private pool with a magnificent view of either the Ayung Valley or paddy fields.

Asmara and Ayung Suites. These are Valley Suites with large private swimming pools and sweeping views of the nearby rice terraces and valley.

Amandari Suites. These suites offer one or two detached bedrooms along with a separate living room pavilion. There is also a private swimming pool and an outdoor bale of teak and bamboo with outstanding views of the Ayung Gorge.

Amandari Villa. The Amandari Villa is nestled in rice terraces by the resort and includes five pavilions: three detached terrace-style bedrooms; a separate living room walled entirely with windows; and a fully fitted kitch- en. A garden and landscaped deck embrace the two-tiered swimming pool. The villa is less than a minute’s drive from Amandari with a vehicle, which is available on call.

FOOD AND DINING The Restaurant, open to the breezes and craft- ed largely of teak wood, overlooks Amandari's swimming pool and the gorge. It serves break- fast, lunch and dinner with delicacies of West- ern and local Indonesian style. The Bar, with its terrace seating, features a wide selection of drinks.

OTHER FACILITIES Library. A broad selection of books, newspa- pers, CDs and games are available in the library. The Library also offers an Internet and e-mail access.

Gift Shop and Gallery. A wide variety of Indone- sian handicrafts, including baskets, silk, pottery and silverware are available.

Amandari’s Spa. is surrounded by water and features two open-air massage bales, a beauty room, sauna and steam room.

The Fitness Centre. is fitted with a fully equipped cardiovascular gym and looks out onto the surrounding lotus pond.

Swimming Pool. Amandari’s signature 32m green-tiled swimming pool.

Tennis. The hard court is available for day and night play. Balls, rackets and tennis shoes are complimentary. A playing partner can also be arranged.
