EMERALDA GOLF CLUB

Many years ago Emeralda, which is strategically located between Jakarta and Bogor used to be an old rubber plantation owned by PT Swadaya Prada Pratama.In the midst of this very peaceful plot of land, Astra Foundation established and managed a training centre, where people learned how to make the best use of the land. It was during a visit to this Astra training centre that Mrs. Judith Soeryadjaya had the idea of completely transforming this old plantation into something more beautiful and useful.

In her visit, Mrs. Judith envisioned a world-class golf complex equipped with the best Golf & Country Club. Within its premises, this complex would have:

1. Two beautiful golf courses specially designed by the world-class golfers;
2. A club house designed in West Java style by a world class architect;
3. Extensive sport and entertainment facilities;
4. A residential complex of excellent quality;
5. A beautiful landscape for the golf courses.

Emeralda estate will indeed be a meticulously planned oasis. It combines beau- tiful scenery, an elegant residential complex and 36-holes world- class golf courses designed by Jack Nicklaus and Arnold Palmer; both of which are the golf legends.

Now and then
What Emeralda has become today is a manifestation of those beautiful visions. With its existence, Emer- alda inspires everyone with fabulous things never found before. A line of new Golf Carts, well-main- tained Driving Range, Restaurant, VIP Room, Spa & Sauna, Pro-shop and a new Business Centers have been designed and added to guarantee ultimate comfort and satisfaction. These facilities are of the first class quality and all efforts are indeed orches- trated to exceed anyone’s expectation.

Now everyone can enjoy beautiful scenery of the verdant hills standing next to blue lakes surrounded by various types of colorful flowers.

Such complex would literally be an oasis for every one, especially golf-lover and golfers who dream to live in an integrated residential complex equipped with all modern amenities and ideally located in vicinity of Jakarta.

The Arnold Palmer River, Lake Course and The Jack Nicklaus Plantation Course have been built with the utmost respect to the topography and environment. The 27 holes have been completed by mid 1995 and the remaining 9 holes by Nicklaus are scheduled for completion shortly. These courses create exciting experiences and challenges, which will test skills of every golfer at every level.
