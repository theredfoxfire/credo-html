BUSINESS UNIT

SRI, T8TOWER - JAKARTA

A NEW LANDMARK FOR JAKARTA

T8 Tower Believes the key success in today’s dynamic global development is the balance between work and lifestyle within a safe and creative environment , where business and social needs are served steps away from office

THE T8 TOWER VISION

As one of jakarta's first and only lifestyle development towers combining serviced Offices and Inner - city Residence. Surya Raya Investama is pleased to launch T8 Tower ( T8T ) - a modern facility for today's professional and dynamic social entrepreneurs designed to maximize productivity while appreciating lifestyle.

PROMIXIMITY

Located stragically within Jakarta’s Central Business District in the area commonly known as “Mampang Golden Triangle”. T8T provides convenient access to the Jakarta’s inner toll road at Jl. Gatot Subroto leading to the Soekarno-Hatta International Airport, including the Jakarta Outer Ring Road (JORR), Sudirman Central Business District (SCBD) and Mega Kuningan Business District - all within 3 km radius from T8 Tower entrance gate.
